What We Do

Here's how you can joing our efforts to a safer tomorrow

Objective

ePark strives to reduce unauthorised roadside parking in order to minimize traffic jams and avoidable collissions.

How do we do it?

We work with owners of parking spaces to allow customers to book their parking spots online. Our parking spots are safe and secure and under the constant watch of security personnel.

Do you have area that can be used as parking space?

Simply partner with us so that we can commercialize your free land. Register through our website and after your area is approved, you can easily make money.

How do I find parking?

You are charged from the moment you arrive at your parking space and the security personnel confirms your license plate number matches the one the parking space was booked for online. You will make your payment once you return to your parking space. You are charged per hour.

Payment

We accept payment in any form (Cash, Debit/Credit cards, PayTM...)

Availability

We are a 24/7 service with parking spaces spread out throughout India. You can park in any available parking space regardless of what time of day it is. Contact us at anytime regarding any questions or concerns you have.
